Slow Cooker Baby Back Ribs

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds baby back ribs, trimmed
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • Â½ onion, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

Directions

  1. Season ribs with salt and pepper.
  2. Pour 1/2 cup water into the slow cooker.
  3. Add ribs to slow cooker.
  4. Scatter onion and garlic over top of ribs.
  5. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours or High for 4 hours.
  6. Transfer ribs from the slow cooker to a baking sheet convex side up.
  7. Discard onion and garlic.
  8. Broil ribs until meat's surface is lightly browned.
  9. Remove ribs from broiler and apply layer of sauce.
  10. Return ribs to broiler until sauce caramelizes.
  11. Remove ribs from broiler and apply 2nd layer of sauce.
  12. Return ribs to broiler until sauce caramelizes.
  13. Repeat for 3rd layer
  14. Remove ribs from boiler and serve.
