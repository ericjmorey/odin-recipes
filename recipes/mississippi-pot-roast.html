Mississippi Pot Roast

1 (3 pound) chuck roast ½ (12 ounce) jar pepperoncini ½ (12 ounce) jar pepperoncini juice 1 (1 ounce) packet au jus gravy mix 1 (0.4 oz) package buttermilk ranch dressing (such as Hidden Valley®) salt and ground black pepper to taste Combine chuck roast, pepperoncini, pepperoncini juice, au jus mix, buttermilk ranch dressing, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker. Cook on Low until roast is fork-tender, about 8 hours. Pull chuck apart with 2 forks. Cover with aluminum foil to keep warm.
