Dr. Pepper Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) pork shoulder roast
  • 2 tablespoons coarse kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle Dr. Pepper
  • 1 (4 fluid ounce) bottle liquid smoke flavoring, or to taste
  • 4 cloves garlic

Directions

  1. Place pork roast in a slow cooker, rub with salt, and pour Dr. Pepper, liquid smoke, and garlic over the top.
  2. Cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours.
  3. Remove pork to a bowl.
  4. Strain liquid through a fine-mesh strainer; reserve liquid.
  5. Add garlic cloves back to to pork.
  6. Shred pork using two forks.
  7. Add portion of liquid to shedded pork to desired moisture.
  8. Serve with your favorite BBQ sauce.
